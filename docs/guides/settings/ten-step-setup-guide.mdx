# 10-step setup guide

Set up your boat rental platform from scratch. Follow these steps in order and you'll be ready to accept bookings.

---

## Step 1: Set your general information

<Button href="https://dashboard.letsbook.app/general-info">General information →</Button>

Fill in your company name, address, contact details, and default language and currency. Add your terms and conditions and privacy policy. This information shows up throughout your booking system and in customer emails.

---

## Step 2: Compose your notifications

<Button href="https://dashboard.letsbook.app/notifications">Configure notifications →</Button>

First, [enable the languages](https://dashboard.letsbook.app/localization) you want to support. Then set up the emails your customers receive: booking confirmations, payment receipts, reminders before their rental, and cancellation messages. Review the default templates and adjust the wording to match your brand.

Read more: [Edit customer notifications](/guides/settings/rental-setups/edit-customer-notifications) · [Languages](/guides/settings/booking-form/languages)

---

## Step 3: Create a dock

<Button href="https://dashboard.letsbook.app/docks/add">Create your first dock →</Button>

A dock is a pickup location where customers collect their boat. Add the name, address, and any instructions customers need (parking info, access codes, who to ask for).

If you operate from multiple locations, create a dock for each one.

Read more: [Manage docks](/guides/settings/manage-docks)

---

## Step 4: Add your boats

<Button href="https://dashboard.letsbook.app/models/add">Add your boats →</Button>

Create a boat model for each type of boat you rent out. Add its name, description, specs, and photos, and specify how many of that model you have. Then assign them to a dock.

Good photos make a big difference. Show the boat from multiple angles, including the interior.

Read more: [Manage boats](/guides/settings/boats/manage-boats) · [Assign boats to docks](/guides/settings/boats/assign-boats-to-docks)

---

## Step 5: Set up schedules

<Button href="https://dashboard.letsbook.app/schedules">Set up schedules →</Button>

There are two types of schedules. Use a [weekly schedule](/guides/settings/rental-setups/schedules/weekly-schedule) if you want customers to freely pick their own start and end time. Use [slots](/guides/settings/rental-setups/schedules/slot-schedule) if you work with fixed departure times or multi-day rentals.

Read more: [Schedules overview](/guides/settings/rental-setups/schedules)

---

## Step 6: Set your pricing

<Button href="https://dashboard.letsbook.app/pricing">Configure pricing →</Button>

Set your hourly or daily rates. You can add seasonal pricing, weekend premiums, and multi-day discounts.

Read more: [Pricing overview](/guides/settings/rental-setups/pricing)

---

## Step 7: Configure booking confirmation

<Button href="https://dashboard.letsbook.app/confirmation-methods">Set confirmation methods →</Button>

Decide how bookings get confirmed:

- **Instant** after payment
- **Manual** where you approve each booking yourself
- **Deposit-based** where customers pay a deposit upfront and the rest later

Read more: [Booking confirmation settings](/guides/settings/rental-setups/booking-confirmation-settings)

---

## Step 8: Create your rental setup

<Button href="https://dashboard.letsbook.app/rental-setup">Build your rental setup →</Button>

This is where everything comes together. A rental setup combines your boats, schedules, pricing, and confirmation method into one bookable flow. Connect the pieces and configure what information you collect from customers during booking.

Read more: [Rental setups](/guides/settings/rental-setups)

---

## Step 9: Customize your styling

<Button href="https://dashboard.letsbook.app/styling">Customize styling →</Button>

Match the booking form to your brand. Upload your logo, pick your colors, and choose fonts.

Read more: [Match your branding](/guides/settings/booking-form/match-branding)

---

## Step 10: Connect your payment provider

<Button href="/guides/settings/payments/receive-payments">Connect payment provider →</Button>

Connect Stripe or Mollie to accept payments. Configure which payment methods you want to offer (credit cards, bank transfers, digital wallets) and test a payment to make sure it works.

Read more: [Stripe](/guides/settings/payments/stripe) · [Mollie](/guides/settings/payments/mollie)

---

## Next steps
