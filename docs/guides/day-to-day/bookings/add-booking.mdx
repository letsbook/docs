# Add a booking manually

## Step 1: Start adding a booking

- From **Bookings** → **[Add Booking](https://dashboard.letsbook.app/bookings/add)** (main method)
- Use the **Quick Add** button in the menu bar, the green button on the dashboard overview or click the icon in the planning overviews

## Step 2: Add the customer

- Existing customer? Type their name and select
- New customer? Click **Add New customer**

<InlineVideoPlayer videoSrc={addCustomerVideo} title="Add a customer" />

## Step 3: Fill booking details

Fill in date, boat, duration, and extras. If the booking comes in via a partner, select them under **Partner** — the booking will automatically apply their pricing and settings.

:::note[Override mode options]
By default, you follow the same rules as customers (availability, duration limits, pricing). Need to break the rules? Turn on **Override mode**.

- **Ignore settings** - Book with different pick-up times or durations than allowed
- **Ignore availability** - Place bookings even when no boats are available
- **Ignore cost changes** - Skip automatic price calculations
  :::

<InlineVideoPlayer videoSrc={ignoreSettingsVideo} title="Use override mode" />

## Step 4: Check and adjust

Your booking is now created but needs confirmation. You can still make manual adjustments (like changing the price) before finalizing.

## Step 5: Confirm the booking

You land on the booking detail page with a **confirmation bar** pinned to the top. It stays visible across every tab, so you can finalize from anywhere.

Click **Confirm** when you're ready.

<InlineVideoPlayer videoSrc={checkAndConfirmVideo} />

:::note[Confirmation options]
Expand the bar to fine-tune what happens on confirm:

- **Notify customer** - Sends the booking confirmation email plus any other trip messages you have configured.
- **Include payment link** - Adds a payment request to the confirmation email for the outstanding amount.
- **Payment due** - Pick when the payment link expires:
    - **Before start of trip** - At pickup time.
    - **Before end of trip** (default) - At dropoff. Lets last-minute walk-ups still pay through the link.
    - **In 2 weeks** - 14 days from now, capped at the trip end.
    - **Custom** - Pick your own date and time.
      :::

:::tip[In override mode]
The bar shows a yellow left border and a "check costs" label. Open the **Finance** tab to enter your manual prices, then come back and confirm.
:::

**What happens next:** The booking flips to confirmed and the bar disappears. From here you can still edit details, add notes, or send fresh payment requests.
